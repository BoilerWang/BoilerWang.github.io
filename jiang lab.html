Welcome to Our Lab

Our lab belongs to Department of Nutrition at Purdue University.

Research Areas

  • We have developed analytical methods for vitamin E metabolomics.
  • We conduct cell-based studies and animal experiments to explore the anti-inflammatory and anti-cancer action of different vitamin E forms and their metabolites.
  • We use mouse models to investigate the role of gut microbiome in anti-cancer effects of different vitamin E forms and their metabolites in colorectal cancer.
